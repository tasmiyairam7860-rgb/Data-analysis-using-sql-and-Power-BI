📚 Books & Orders Data Analysis Project
Project Overview
This project demonstrates a complete data cleaning, analysis, and visualization pipeline using MySQL and Power BI.
The dataset includes three tables:
Table      Rows
Books      500
Customers  1,000
Orders     1,000

Goals:
Ensure data quality in all tables
Explore sales, revenue, and customer behavior
Generate interactive dashboards in Power BI for insights

Tools & Technologies
MySQL – Data cleaning & analysis
Power BI – Dashboard & visualization
SQL – Querying

Data Cleaning :
Performed using MySQL:
. Removed missing and invalid records
. Handled null values and inconsistencies
. Standardized formats (dates, price, phone, etc.)
. Ensured referential integrity between tables
📂 Full scripts available in the sql/ folder.

Analysis Performed
Key analysis includes:
. Sales and revenue calculation
. Books sold by genre and author
. Customer order behavior
. Most frequently ordered books
. High-value customers and locations
. Stock remaining after orders



Power BI Dashboard
I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze and visualize bookstore sales performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights into yearly trends, top‑selling products, and customer behavior, enabling data‑driven decisions for inventory and marketing strategies.

Key Features & Visualizations*
- Books sold yearly*: A line chart showing the annual sum of orders, highlighting sales growth over time.
- Top selling books*: A bar chart displaying the count of each bestselling title, helping identify high‑demand products.
- Books sold by genre*: A line graph that maps genre‑wise sales, useful for genre‑based inventory planning.
- Top 5 customers*: A bar chart listing the most frequent buyers, aiding in loyalty program targeting.
- Customer segment*: A pie chart splitting customers into regular, loyal, and new segments with percentage breakdowns.
- KPI cards*: Metrics for total active customers (1,000), average orders per customer (4.73), total books sold (14K), and total revenue ($1.36 M).

Interactive Elements
- Slicers for *Country*, *City*, *Order date*, and *Genre* let users filter data dynamically.
- Date range slider enables time‑based analysis of sales trends.

Business Impact
The dashboard empowers the bookstore to spot bestselling genres and customers, optimize stock, and tailor marketing campaigns, ultimately driving revenue growth and improving customer engagement.


Insights

    - Total revenue is $1.36M from 14K books sold.
    - Fiction and Non-Fiction genres drive most sales.
    - 8.39% revenue comes from *loyal* customers.
    - _“Animal Industry” ,Lawyer_indicate ,“Mrs. rich”_ are bestsellers.
    - Top 3 Fantasy books are priced >$150, targeting premium audience.
    - Remaining stock shows which books need restocking.
    - Some books have low stock, need urgent replenishment.
    - Cities like Alexandrabury and Aguilarstad contribute high sales.
    - Targeted marketing needed in these regions.
